HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY

Headmistress: Professor Minerva McGonagall

To Mr. Devon San, in the upstairs-left chamber,

It is my great pleasure to inform you that you have been chosen to undertake a most curious Trial of Admission to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Your journey begins September 17th at precisely 3:30 p.m., when your passage across the seas shall depart, carrying you to a city alive with enchantments and bustling with both wizard and Muggle alike.

The trial will test your wit, courage, and adaptability. You will wander through grand halls of enchantments where moving pictures breathe life into every corridor. You shall prove your spirit among roaring crowds, where banners wave and voices thunder in unison. Feasts rich with spice and fire await, served with the warm laughter of companions. And no trial would be complete without the golden glow of taverns, where ales flow freely and secrets are whispered late into the night. Beware: each challenge shall be revealed in its own time, and not a moment sooner.

To prepare, you must break the seal below. Therein you will find instructions on what to carry for your forthcoming weekend. Follow them closely, for only then will you be ready to face the surprises set before you.

Yours most sincerely,

Professor Minerva McGonagall
Deputy Headmistress

Packing Instructions

Devon San, heed these hints well, for they are the keys to thriving in your trial:

  • Garments sturdy enough for long days of wandering and sudden bursts of excitement.
  • Cloth that can shift from daylight errands to evening feasts with ease.
  • Footwear that grants both swiftness and endurance upon uneven ground.
  • A trusted device for capturing memories and glimpses of magic.
  • Proof of your identity and right of passage across the seas.

Prepare these well, and let curiosity be your compass.

Further surprises shall unfold only in their proper hour...
